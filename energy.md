### Metric explanation

- Calorie deficit is defined by calories in (food) minus calories out (basal + burned).
- Over time I have noticed that, taken at face value, the calorie deficits implied by my raw data are implausibly large. I therefore increase calorie intake by a multiple that rationalises my weight changes over time (trial and error, not principled/cross-validated prediction of changes over time). This is then multiplied by a function of the ratio of time between date and present day and time between earliest date and present day (because I think my tracking has been getting slightly more accurate over time)
